About Us

D&J Heating Solution carries European Standard Technology for room heating and instant hot water supply form LPG sourced Combination Boiler which is equiped with various Safety Controls to protect from unwanted breakdowns and accidental hazards. This heating technology is widely used in the UK, Europe, the US, South Korea and Japan, and its safety standards are accepted by the UK and European Safety Departments.

It's a simple rectangle machine which consists of Flow & Return pipes, outlet pipe, incoming cold water pipe, hot water supply pipe, gas pipe & safety discharge pipe. This Technology which heats up the room with steel panel radiator and supplies instant hot water on demand is extremely popular in the UK and is used in 99% of the residential homes. These boilers comes with the digital display panel which shows fault codes whenever the boiler has technicial problems. And each fault code represents different faults with the boiler and tells you how to fix it. It has very low maintenance cost and it is very energy efficient.

This technology was first introduced in Nepal by Dhanu Thakali & Jagan Gurung who work as registerd gas engineer in the UK. This system has been sucessfully installed and tested at the Nepal Tourism & Hotel Management Collage in Pokhara and a fews homes in Kathmandu and Pokhara.

Technology

Central Heating System:

Central Heating is describe as a system in which several rooms are centrally heated by combination boiler & heat emitter called radiator where heated hot water from the boiler circulates by a pump as requested by programmer & room stat. Programmer can be in built in or externally installed and room stat which senses the room temperature is fixed on the wall. When the room stat satisfies the ambient temperature requested its shuts the boiler down. Each and every radiator is also controlled by a valve called thermostatic radiator valve which closes the heated water supply in the radiator after it reaches the demanding temperature resulting saving the fuel. Liquid Petroleum Gas (LPG) is used to run the combination boiler. Consumption of LPG is depends upon size of the boiler in KW input & running time. Thumb Rule: Boiler with 18KW heat Input with 4 double convector 1000X600mm radiators if runs for continuous 4 hours a day 13.5kgs LPG cylinder will roughly last for two weeks. Central heating pipe works can be copper or plastic composite pipe. All the copper pipeline is installed with lead free soldered joint. Fumes from the boiler are externally discharged with the metal pipe called flue with air intake built in.

Flow & Return Pipeline:

Flow is the pipe line from the boiler which circulates heated water by the boiler to radiator with the help of pump. Return is the pipe from the heat emitter radiator which runs towards boiler to reheat. A circulating pump which is inside the boiler circulates the flow and return water in the pipe lines. Water in the flow & return pipe is always be there and does not cross contaminate with cold water. In case of pressure drops it needs recharging. Water pressure in flow & return needs to be at least 1 bar or in the green zone in the pressure gauge at the bottom of the boiler.

Hot Water Supply:

Hot water supplies from the boiler when hot water tap is turned on & when the tap is turned off hot water terminates. Temperature of hot water can be increased or decreased from the boiler control. Time to discharge hot water depends upon how far the boiler is installed from the tap. Solar Hot Water can be tapped in to system so when there is solar hot water available the boiler does not need to run which will be controlled by motorized valve.
